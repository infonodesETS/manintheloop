Man in the Loop

An experimental research to map companies working either as manufacturers or investors in the Defence field, in Europe and abroad.

01 /
Company Search →
Look up any individual company or investor in the database. View funding, founders, sector, investors, portfolio connections.
02 /
Supply Chain →
Map companies, investors, and their relationships across Defence · Mining · Tech · Startup sectors.
about this research

Identifying who truly holds the reins of autonomous weapons research and production.

  • Defence Companies that produce weapons systems, military platforms, and defence technologies
  • Mining Extraction of critical raw materials for defence: semiconductors, rare earths, strategic metals
  • Tech Technology companies providing AI, cloud, and infrastructure to the military sector
