About
Man in the Loop is an experimental open-data research project mapping companies and investors in the defence sector — tracking who finances whom across the global defence supply chain, with a focus on European Defence Fund beneficiaries (2021–2027).

Methodology, data sources, known issues, and team information will be documented here.
